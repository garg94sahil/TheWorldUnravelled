Four things worth knowing

01

DTC pivot

Direct-to-consumer now accounts for 50% of revenue, up from 31% three years ago. DTC grew 11% in FY2025 — the engine of the whole strategy.

02

The women's gap

Women are 36% of Levi's sales but half the world's population. CEO Michelle Gass has set a clear target: 50%. The women's business grew 11% annually in FY2025.

03

Asia's promise

India is Levi's 6th largest global market and #1 in Asia, with revenue up 50% vs pre-pandemic levels. Europe grew 14% in early 2025. International is now a tariff buffer.

04

The $10B problem

At guided 4–5% annual growth, reaching the $10 billion target takes 15–20 years. The original 2027 deadline was quietly dropped. The strategy is right; the timeline isn't.
