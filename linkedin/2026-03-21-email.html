Four things worth knowing

01

Scale wins

Email has 4.83 billion users — 56% of the entire global population. Slack has 38.8 million daily active users. The scale isn't even close.

02

AI's home base

Google, Grammarly, Notion, and Yahoo all made email their top AI investment in 2025. The inbox is the richest structured dataset of your professional life.

03

You own it

Email is the only channel no platform can take from you. When Meta shut down Workplace, every conversation vanished overnight. That can't happen to your inbox.

04

Newsletters are booming

Substack hit $1.1B valuation on 5M paid subscribers. Beehiiv serves 350M monthly readers. People aren't just tolerating email — they're paying for it.
